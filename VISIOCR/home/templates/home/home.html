About ViSi OCR

ViSi OCR is a robust visitor pass system designed to streamline visitor management processes. It allows you to generate, validate, and manage visitor passes efficiently.

Features

  • Scan QR Code for pass validation
  • Generate visitor passes with detailed information
  • Download visitor passes in PDF format
  • View and manage visitor pass records
